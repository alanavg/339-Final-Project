What's your skin type?

Knowing what type of skin type you have is vital to understand your skin's needs and whether the products you are using are right for you! Not knowing what your skin type is can lead to using skincare products that are doing more harm than good.

Click the buttons below to explore your skin type and products :)

Normal Skin Type

~ Normal skin type is not too oily and not too dry
 
~ The T-zone area (forehead, chin, and nose) may experience some oiliness, but not an excessive amount
 
~ Those with a normal skin type may experience seasonal dryness and oiliness
 
~ Although normal skin types don't struggle with dryness or oiliness, it's important to have a hydrating and gentle skin care routine to avoid skin barrier damage and dehydrated skin :)
